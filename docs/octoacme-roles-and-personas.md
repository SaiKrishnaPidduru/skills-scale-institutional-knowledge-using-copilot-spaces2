# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create user-centered designs and advocate for usability throughout the project lifecycle. They collaborate with Product Managers, Developers, and QA to ensure solutions are intuitive and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Design user experience flows, wireframes, and prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Advocate for user needs and accessibility standards
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation feasibility
- Participate in design reviews and provide feedback on feature completeness
- Document design systems and interaction patterns

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user confusion and support burden
- Maintain consistency across product interfaces
- Incorporate user feedback into iterative improvements

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with cross-functional teams
- Wireframes and prototypes in design tools
- Accessibility and usability guidelines in documentation
- Weekly syncs with Product and Development leads

### Interactions
- Works with **Product Managers** on requirements clarification and priority
- Collaborates with **Developers** on feasibility and implementation details
- Partners with **QA** on acceptance criteria related to UX
- Influences **Project Managers** on timeline considerations for design phases

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define metrics, track outcomes, and design experiments to measure product and project success. They partner with Product Managers and Developers to instrument analytics and provide data-driven insights.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and KPIs aligned with project goals
- Design and implement analytics instrumentation
- Conduct data analysis and create dashboards for stakeholder visibility
- Design and run A/B tests and experiments
- Provide insights to inform prioritization decisions
- Document data methodologies and measurement approaches

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across the team
- Measure and demonstrate product impact
- Identify optimization opportunities through analytics
- Support iterative improvements based on evidence

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews with Product and Project Managers
- Dashboard updates and analytics reports
- Experiment proposals and results documentation
- Data methodology guides and measurement frameworks

### Interactions
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on success metrics definition
- Works with **Developers** to integrate analytics SDKs and event tracking
- Provides insights to influence **Project Managers** on prioritization
- Reports to stakeholders on project outcomes and impact

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines, ensure system reliability, and streamline deployment processes. They work with Developers and QA to automate testing and enable rapid, safe releases.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment infrastructure
- Implement automated testing and quality gates in deployment
- Monitor system health, performance, and incident response
- Manage infrastructure as code and environment configuration
- Collaborate with Developers on release strategies and rollback procedures
- Provide guidance on observability and logging

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, and reliable deployments
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Reduce manual operational overhead
- Improve system observability and incident response

### Typical Communication
- Deployment runbooks and incident playbooks
- Infrastructure and pipeline documentation
- Standups with Development and QA teams
- Post-incident reviews and continuous improvement sessions

### Interactions
- Supports **Developers** with deployment tooling and CI/CD guidance
- Works with **QA** on automated testing in deployment pipelines
- Collaborates with **Project Managers** on release timing and risks
- Escalates production incidents to relevant teams

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads act as the primary point of contact for user-reported issues and feedback. They feed customer insights into the planning process and ensure critical problems are escalated and resolved quickly.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and manage user-reported issues and support tickets
- Identify patterns in support requests and escalate systemic issues
- Feed customer feedback and insights into Product and Project planning
- Coordinate communication with customers during incidents
- Document known issues and workarounds
- Contribute to knowledge base and help documentation

### Goals
- Deliver responsive, high-quality customer support
- Reduce support volume through proactive issue resolution
- Influence product decisions based on customer needs
- Improve customer satisfaction and retention

### Typical Communication
- Weekly support metrics reviews with Product and Project Managers
- Customer feedback summaries and trend reports
- Escalations for critical or recurring issues
- Collaboration on release notes and known issues documentation

### Interactions
- Provides customer insights to **Product Managers** for prioritization
- Reports technical issues to **Developers** for resolution
- Escalates critical problems to **Project Managers** and **DevOps Engineers**
- Advocates for customer needs in project planning discussions

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads review code and deployments for security risks, own incident response processes, and provide guidance on secure development practices. They ensure security considerations are integrated throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security code reviews and vulnerability assessments
- Define and enforce security standards and compliance requirements
- Implement security testing in CI/CD pipelines
- Lead security incident response and post-incident reviews
- Provide secure development training and guidance
- Maintain security documentation and threat models

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities and data breaches
- Build a security-aware development culture
- Enable rapid incident response and recovery
- Maintain compliance with regulatory and organizational standards

### Typical Communication
- Security review checkpoints in deployment process
- Vulnerability reports and remediation guidance
- Security incident playbooks and response procedures
- Training sessions and secure coding guidelines

### Interactions
- Reviews work from **Developers** on security best practices
- Collaborates with **DevOps Engineers** on secure infrastructure
- Escalates critical security issues to **Project Managers** and leadership
- Works with **QA** on security testing methodology

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts map business processes and requirements, ensuring project goals align with organizational strategy. They clarify and document acceptance criteria and bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct business process analysis and requirements gathering
- Document business requirements and acceptance criteria
- Facilitate stakeholder discussions to clarify scope and priorities
- Ensure project goals align with organizational strategy
- Validate solutions against business requirements
- Create traceability matrices between requirements and implementation

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between business strategy and project delivery
- Reduce scope creep and misalignment through clear requirements
- Enable stakeholder confidence in project outcomes
- Support business case validation and ROI measurement

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and process flow diagrams
- Stakeholder meetings and clarification sessions
- Acceptance criteria reviews with delivery teams
- Business case updates and alignment reports

### Interactions
- Partners with **Product Managers** on business requirements and strategy alignment
- Clarifies requirements with **Developers** and **QA** on acceptance criteria
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** on scope and stakeholder expectations
- Supports **Data Analysts** on success metrics tied to business outcomes
